About Us

electronic spare parts and factory machines

CV. Anugerah Agung Utama operates in the field of machine installation and pallet manufacturing. We have expertise in overcoming various obstacles and challenges related to heavy equipment, industrial machines, shipping, automotive spare parts, as well as making quality pallets. With extensive experience, we are able to provide effective and efficient engineering solutions.

We serve a variety of customers, including factories, government agencies, schools, universities, and various other entities. We customize our solutions to meet the unique needs of each customer, with the goal of improving their operational efficiency and success
